Company Water Mission

In Ukraine, to move water and make it clean for children who drink the water and swim in the river.

As an American company, and in conformance with the Budapest Memorandum, we propose to assist Ukraine in defending the country, winning the war to the 1991 internationally recognized boundaries, rebuild the country damaged by the war and upgrade the water environment in conformance with EU standards to facilitate Ukraine's entrance into the Union.

To provide engineering design, finance, construction and operation for wastewater, potable water, stormwater and other projects plus environmental monitoring in Ukraine to facilitate compliance with European Union (EU) environmental standards.

To construct water facilities that have zero detrimental impact on the environment, no cost to the rate payer and zero or negative greenhouse gas footprint by using best available proven technology and by incorporating green energy production into our treatment and pumping projects. Green energy production will be mostly solar, wind, biogas balanced by cleaned biogas storage, batteries and pumped storage.

In a dual, military/civilian use manner, to assist the Ukrainian military in the design and construction of defensive fortifications, river crossings, roads and air/water/land drones integrated with Artificial Intelligence (AI). For example, construction equipment and techniques used for military fortification construction can clearly be used for water, sewer and other utility construction. Drone technology for military use can be used for environmental monitoring integrated with surface water and other environmental mathematical modeling.

But our prime military activity is sea drones due to our location on the Black Sea front lines throughout the war, our hydraulic capabilities, AI knowledge and experience, contacts with the Ukrainian Navy and merchant marine, development of contacts with the navies of various countries.

Marketing

Our sales team of Natalie Poloz and Olga Kameneva are local professional women who have stayed living, taking care of their children and working in Kherson throughout the war including the initial battle for the city, Russian military occupation, liberation, the Kakhovka Dam flood and daily artillery and drone bombardment. They have extensive local contacts in government, the military (Ms. Kameneva is a captain in the Ukrainian Naval reserves), medical field and humanitarian aid. Ms. Poloz is a certified medical professional, and experienced medical sales person with medical banking experience who managed one of the World Central Kitchen food distribution centers after liberation. Ms. Kameneva has contacts with local media and is a certified English interpreter and simultaneous translator so all press releases, marketing materials and presentations can be entirely in flawless Ukrainian for highest sales impact. The sales team works under the general direction of Donald Flett who makes the formal presentations to government bodies.

We have identified and calculated the cost to address water and wastewater issues in the 460 largest cities and towns in Ukraine and to meet the EU environmental water standards. Our sales team has started to contacting the relevant government officials in these cities and towns to offer an initial analysis of their needs and to present a formal proposal for a conceptual engineering, financial, and environmental evaluation with the estimated cost per home per month for water and sewer service.

Markets

Water, wastewater and stormwater facilities for Ukrainian cities and municipalities, industry, agriculture, irrigation, contaminated sites and hydroelectric power generation. Water drones for automated monitoring and cleanup particularly of bottom sediments. Geographical markets are Ukraine, US and Europe particularly if we can be quicker, cheaper and better than other engineering companies. Advantages are lower salaries for high grade technical personnel in Ukraine, the latent technical talent here and the push that the war has given to local innovations.

Military

Mine detection and removal in water and on land. High speed underwater drones and torpedoes using the super cavitation principal and pre-programmed navigation technique. Construction of defensive fortifications and river crossings. Rapid English instructions for Ukrainian military to facilitate use of western weapons. Integration of AI into new weapons systems particularly drones.
