Water Mission Ukraine

Delivering clean water and rebuilding Ukraine's water infrastructure through sustainable and EU-compliant technologies.

Mission

In Ukraine, to move water and make it clean for children who drink the water and swim in the river. As an American company, and in conformance with the Budapest Memorandum, we propose to assist Ukraine in defending the country, winning the war to the 1991 internationally recognized boundaries, rebuild the country damaged by the war and upgrade the water environment in conformance with EU standards to facilitate Ukraine's entrance into the Union.

Ukraine Strategy

To provide engineering design, finance, construction and operation for wastewater, potable water, stormwater and other projects plus environmental monitoring in Ukraine to facilitate compliance with European Union (EU) environmental standards.

EU Environmental Standards

We aim to construct water facilities that have zero detrimental impact on the environment, no cost to the rate payer and zero or negative greenhouse gas footprint by using best available proven technology and incorporating green energy production into our treatment and pumping projects.

Zero Environmental Impact

Green energy production will be mostly solar, wind, biogas balanced by cleaned biogas storage, batteries and pumped storage. Our goal is environmental balance and sustainable infrastructure.

Dual Military/Civil Use

In a dual, military/civilian use manner, we assist the Ukrainian military in the design and construction of defensive fortifications, river crossings, roads, and air/water/land drones integrated with water infrastructure projects.
